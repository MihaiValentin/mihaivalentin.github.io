Frequently asked questions (FAQ)

  • Who is this book for?

    This book is primarily written for software engineers, engineering managers, software architects, directors of engineering and even CTOs. In many sections in the book, I described how to implement various improvements based on your role (eg: for data-driven development, a software engineer should track all the metrics and logs, but the director of engineering role is the one that should introduce an unified platform for data collection at analytics for the whole company). In this way, even if you are a software engineer right now, you will be able to use the techniques described in the book now and in the future when you will get promoted to a higher position.

  • How will this book help me?

    This book will help you fast track your IT career. It will give you actionable advice on how to improve your mindset, your soft skills and your tech skills. As a result of that, you will be able to improve the projects you are working on, deliver faster, unblock yourself and others, and better support the company you are working for. If you apply the advice in the book, you will be starting to work smart instead of hard, be ready to take higher paid jobs, and save yourself a lot of time that it normally takes to grow all by yourself.

  • How deep does the book go?

    This ebook is meant to give you the "a-ha" moment. Its purpose is to give you a boost in your growth, by pointing you in the right directions. I realy value your time and every page and section in this ebook has meaningful, actionable information that can help you right away.
